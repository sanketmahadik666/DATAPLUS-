**REGULATORY COMPLIANCE CIRCULAR**
Circular ID: COMP-2024-012
Issue Date: March 7, 2024
Effective Date: April 1, 2024

**Subject:** Updated GST Compliance Requirements for Procurement

**Dear Procurement Team,**

The Ministry of Finance has issued new guidelines regarding GST compliance for all procurement activities. Please ensure strict adherence to the following requirements:

**New Requirements:**
1. **E-Invoicing Mandate:** All invoices above ₹10,000 must be e-invoiced
2. **HSN Code Verification:** Verify 6-digit HSN codes for all purchases
3. **Reverse Charge Mechanism:** Applicable for services from unregistered vendors
4. **TDS Compliance:** 2% TDS on payments above ₹30,000 to vendors

**Implementation Timeline:**
- Phase 1 (April 1-15): System updates and testing
- Phase 2 (April 16-30): Staff training and documentation
- Phase 3 (May 1 onwards): Full implementation

**Action Items:**
• Update vendor master data with GSTIN validation
• Modify PO templates to include HSN codes
• Train procurement staff on new requirements
• Update payment processing workflows

**Penalties for Non-Compliance:**
- Late filing: ₹200 per day
- Incorrect returns: ₹10,000 minimum
- Non-registration: ₹10,000 per month
